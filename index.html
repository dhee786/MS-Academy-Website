About Us

Shaping Bright Futures Since 2023 ✨

MS Academy (Mind Sharper Academy) is a premier English medium tuition center located at Gandhi Hath, near Nagar Panchayat, Maner. Established in 2023, we are committed to providing quality education for students from Nursery to Class 10.

Our aim is to make every child a confident learner through engaging teaching methods, personal attention, and a fun learning environment that inspires curiosity and creativity.

The Visionary Behind MS Academy

Founder & Director

With a deep passion for education and a vision to transform lives through quality learning, our Director established MS Academy in 2023. Under their leadership, the academy has grown into a trusted name for English medium education in Maner.

Their commitment to every student's success drives the academy's mission of making education accessible, engaging, and impactful for children from Nursery to Class 10.
